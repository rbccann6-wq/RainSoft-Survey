## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

#### Contact Information
- **Email address** - For employee login and survey contact management
- **Phone number** - For survey customer contacts and employee communication
- **Name** (First and Last) - For employee profiles and survey customers
- **Physical address** - For survey appointments and store location tracking

#### Location Data
- **Precise location** (GPS coordinates) - For clock in/out verification and ensuring employees are at the correct retail location
- **Coarse location** (Store selection) - For determining which retail location employee is working at

#### Photos & Signatures
- **User-uploaded photos** - Clock in verification photos and employee profile pictures
- **Signature images** - Customer survey signatures and employee onboarding document signatures (W-4, I-9, etc.)

#### Identifiers
- **User ID** - Unique employee identifier for authentication and data association
- **Device ID** - For activity tracking and session management

#### Usage Data
- **App interactions** - Page views, button clicks, feature usage
- **Activity heartbeats** - Periodic signals to detect if employee is actively using the survey kiosk
- **Performance data** - Error logs, crash reports, sync status

#### Work-Related Data
- **Employment status** - Active, inactive, invited, terminated
- **Work schedule** - Shift times, dates, store assignments, availability preferences
- **Performance metrics** - Surveys completed per hour, hours worked, appointments set
- **Compensation data** - Base hourly rate, bonuses, quota requirements

#### Sensitive Data
- **Government ID** - Driver's license uploads for employment verification (I-9 compliance)
- **Financial information** - Direct deposit account details, W-4 tax withholding information, I-9 employment authorization

### 1.2 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

- **Salesforce** - Survey sync status, duplicate detection, lead/account information
- **Zapier** - Appointment scheduling confirmations
- **ADP Workforce** - Payroll and time tracking synchronization
- **Google Places API** - Address autocomplete and geocoding for appointments

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Active Employees

Data is retained while your employment is active and for the following periods after termination:

- **Payroll records** (time entries, compensation): 7 years (IRS requirement)
- **Tax documents** (W-4, I-9): 7 years (federal law)
- **Survey data**: Indefinitely (business records)
- **Employment documents**: 3 years after termination (EEOC requirement)

### 4.2 Customer Survey Data

- **Survey responses**: Retained indefinitely for CRM purposes
- **Appointment data**: Retained until customer opts out or requests deletion

### 4.3 Deletion Requests

See Section 6 (Your Rights) for information on requesting data deletion.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in transit** - All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at rest** - Database encryption via OnSpace Cloud
- **Access controls** - Role-based permissions (employee vs. admin)
- **Authentication** - Secure login with email verification

### 5.2 Organizational Safeguards
- **Employee training** - Security and privacy best practices
- **Access logging** - Activity monitoring for suspicious behavior
- **Incident response** - Procedures for data breach notification

### 5.3 Offline Storage
- **Local device storage** - Surveys saved locally (encrypted) until network available
- **Failsafe backups** - Multi-layer redundancy to prevent data loss

**Note**: While we use reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Access & Correction

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal information stored in the App
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Update** contact information, availability, and preferences

**How to exercise**: Log into the App → Profile → Edit your information

### 6.2 Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data.

**How to delete your account**:
1. Open the App
2. Go to **Profile** → **Account Management**
3. Tap **Delete My Account**
4. Confirm deletion (this action is permanent)

**What happens when you delete your account**:
- ✅ Immediate deletion of: Profile info, login credentials, app preferences
- ✅ Scheduled deletion (30 days): Survey history, messages, documents
- ❌ Retained for legal compliance: Payroll records, tax documents (per retention policy)

**Cannot be deleted**: Data required by law (tax forms, payroll records) per Section 4.
